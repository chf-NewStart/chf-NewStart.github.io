Your texts

Paste a chapter from a copy you own — or upload a PDF / .txt — to read it here with paragraph-level notes. The file is read entirely in your browser and never uploaded. On Chrome/Edge, one click translates it line by line on-device in the direction picked above (built-in browser translator — the text never leaves your machine); elsewhere, use the link under each paragraph or paste your own translation.

Texts and notes persist in this browser automatically. The backup file lets you move them to another browser, device, or computer.
