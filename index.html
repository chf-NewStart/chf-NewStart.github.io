Building Smart Evacuation Maps

Integrated LLMs with Dijkstra's algorithm and Ant Colony Optimization (ACO)-inspired routing to design an intelligent evacuation system.

  • It combines the advantages of Dijkstra and ACO respectively
  • Consistently outperforming decisions made by simple Dijkstra in "majority evacuation" (when 90% evacuees are safe)
  • Consistently outperforming ACO in "full evacuation" (when all evacuees are safe)
