Based on Attached — Amir Levine & Rachel S.F. Heller

Every one of us has an attachment style — a consistent pattern of how we think, feel, and behave in close relationships. Developed by researchers Bartholomew, Ainsworth, and Shaver, the model places you on two axes: Anxiety (how much you worry about your relationships) and Avoidance (how comfortable you are with closeness and dependence). This quiz — inspired by the work in Attached — will plot exactly where you land.
